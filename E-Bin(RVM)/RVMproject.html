E-Bin is an AI-based smart waste bin, designed for public places, enabling them to simplify recycling. It sorts and compresses the waste automatically, controls the fill level and processes all the data for convenient waste management.

E-BIN

A machine that will be capable of detecting plastic/metal and will shred it into relevant bin. Giving points on the bottle type and weight. Interactive display is attached for training.

AI-BASED RECOGNITION

Just throw the waste inside! E-Bin will use an AI-based recognition system to identify it, reaching a world leading accuracy of over 92%.

FILL LEVEL CONTROL

Fill level sensors check whether the bin needs to be emptied. You will get an alert of your phone when the bins are full.

PLASTIC COMPRESSION

Plastic get compressed inorder to decrease the volume and lower the frequency of bin emptying.

REDEEM VIA APP

Use your points to give back to those in need so that they can get benefit - your points your choice!!

REAL TIME DATA MANAGEMENT

All the data that E-Bin collects is transferred and processed on a cloud in real-time.

APPS FOR STATS & REPORTS

Our app provides you detailed statistics about the waste you collected, data for optimizing waste collection and the technical status your devices.

WORRY FREE

We manage the pickup and disposal processes with full responsibility and effectively.

GET DETAILED INSIGHTS ON YOUR
SUSTAINABILITY PERFORMANCE

Our app provides information about the fill-level of your E-Bin devices and notifies you when they need to be emptied. It also gives you detailed statistics and reports about waste production, taking CSR reporting to a whole new level

INSERT PLASTIC BOTTLE

Insert bottle into reverse vending machine and bottle cap into the repective area and wait for the bottle to be compressed then insert other bottles.

Proceed to keypad and enter your contact details.

Press * for incentive and enter your 11 digit phone number if you have mistakenly entered wrong number then again press * to clear the screen.
